Juan's Flying Burrito

Juan's Flying Burrito is a New Orleans based Mexican taqueria that offers an affordable menu along with a full bar. Service is fast and efficient, and has a great family atmosphere. Take out included. An Uber Eats partner.

Turkey and the Wolf

Turkey and the Wolf offers a creative sandwhich menu with a full bar. Quick and affordable, Chef Mason Hereford brings a twist to the sandwhich industry in New Orleans. Happy Hour every day 3PM-6PM!

Kin

Kin is a New American and Japanese fine dining restaurant located at 4600 Washington Ave. For a very reasonable rate, try the Chicken and Oxtail Ramen or any of the other amazing dishes prepared with fresh, local ingredients. They offer BYOB as an option for alcohol with no corkage fee. Take out not available.

Shaya

Shaya combines Israeli upbringing with Southern flavors and modern techniques, striking a balance between innovation and tradition. Enjoy a full service bar and the ambience of a restaurant recognized by the James Beard Organization as 2016's Best New Restaurant in America. Take out not available.

Lilette

Lilette is a contemporary French and Italian restaurant.The Main Dining Room features beautiful banquettes and red-wine colored walls to create a romantic ambiance. The outdoor patio is reminiscent of traditional New Orleans courtyards, with stone pavers, Sweet Olive and live Oak trees. Craft cocktails and a full list of wines is offered. Take out unavailable.

August

August is fine dining establishment located in the Central Business District of New Orleans. The elegant architecture of August gives this John Besh restaurant tha alure to get you in, but Chef Todd Pulsinelli's menu keeps patrons coming back. Take out not included.
